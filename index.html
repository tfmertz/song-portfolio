  • Bridge

    A song a created during my studio internship at college. All the sounds come from Pro Tools 10 plug ins. The goal of this song was to have two dueling melodies that complemented eachother and fused into one idea.

  • Ode To Alex

    I created this song in FL Studio 11. All of the sounds I engineered using various synthesizers in FL Studio. The song is tributed to my good friend Alex, who got me into making electronic songs on Thanksgiving Day many years ago. I went home and stayed up to 3am making songs that night! This melody mimics those original songs.

  • Full Of Wonder

    My newest piece, I started out with an idea of what seeing something really awesome for the first time would sound like. I tried to capture that feeling in a melody. Again, all of the sounds are engineered by myself in FL Studio.

  • Bounce

    A very old song that I made some years ago. I had this melody in my head for months and it kept coming back. I figured if I finally turned it into a song then I could get it out of my head, it worked! This song is the best remaining example I have of my early works.

  • Davish

    Originally created in REAPER, I made Davish as a tribute to my friend Dave's style of composition. Sometimes composers get stuck in certain harmonic patterns and it's fun to examine other artists' works to get a fresh perspective. I was pleased with the result and am currently finishing up the rest of this song in FL Studio.

  • Day And Night

    In this song, I explored the concept of two contrasting sections throughout the song. The first section has a busy melody with plenty of pep. There is some foreshadowing of the next section in the harmonic content. Finally, I finish with delicate, hushed tones symbolizing night. The first song that has my vocals on it!
